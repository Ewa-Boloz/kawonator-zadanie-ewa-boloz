CLEANING THE Filter holder AND THE Portafilter

The filter holder in the KW100 must be cleaned every 50 servings.

  1. Remove the Portafilter from the Brew head.
  2. Dismantle the Portafilter to separate the filter holder.
  3. Soak the Filter holder in hot water with a few drops of Kawonator-compatible filter cleaning fluid .
  4. Use a damp cloth to wipe the exterior and interior of the Portafilter and the Brew head.
  5. Wipe the filter holder and re-assemble the Portafilter.
  6. Run water through the KW100 with an empty Portafilter connected to the brew head (with the Filter holder, but without any ground coffee) to rinse out any residual filter-cleaning fluid.
